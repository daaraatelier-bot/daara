Good to Know

Your questions, answered

What is DAARA?
DAARA is an Indian e-commerce fashion marketplace built exclusively for independent and boutique designers. We connect talented designers directly with buyers who appreciate craft — with lower commissions and better visibility than any existing platform.
Is it free to join as a designer?
Yes — joining DAARA as a founding designer is completely free. We only earn when you earn, through our commission starting at 15%.
When does DAARA launch?
We are currently in our pre-launch phase, onboarding our founding set of designers. The platform will go live soon. Designers who join early will get priority placement and founding member benefits.
Who can join DAARA?
Any independent designer, boutique label, or emerging creative across India — whether you design bridal, fusion, pret, menswear, or accessories. If you create with craft and intention, DAARA is for you.
How do I get in touch?
Reach us directly at daara.atelier@gmail.com.
